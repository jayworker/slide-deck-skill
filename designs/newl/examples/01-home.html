NEWL · Energy Materials Lab

Next-generation energy materials
for a sustainable future

We design, synthesize and characterize advanced electrode and electrolyte materials for batteries, fuel cells and solar energy conversion.

Research Areas

Three core directions of the lab, from materials discovery to device integration.

Next-gen Batteries

Solid-state electrolytes and high-capacity cathodes for safer, denser energy storage.

Solar Conversion

Perovskite and tandem architectures pushing photovoltaic efficiency limits.

Green Hydrogen

Electrocatalysts for efficient water splitting and fuel-cell energy conversion.
